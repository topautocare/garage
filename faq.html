General

Booking & Services

Do I need an appointment?
While we accept walk-ins, booking an appointment ensures you get priority service and reduces wait times. You can book easily via our booking page.
Can I track my service progress?
Yes! Log in to your dashboard to view the real-time status of your vehicle repair.

Payments

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Cash, M-Pesa, and major Credit/Debit cards (Visa & Mastercard).
Is there a deposit required?
For standard services, payment is due upon completion. For major repairs or parts orders exceeding KSh 50,000, a deposit may be required.
