Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Top Autocare Garage ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Note: We comply with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019, and other applicable privacy laws.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide our automotive services effectively:

  • Personal Details: Name, email address, and phone number when you create an account.
  • Vehicle Data: Make, model, year, license plate, and VIN to track service history.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you use our website (via Google Analytics).

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly for legitimate business purposes:

  • Processing appointments and vehicle service requests.
  • Sending service reminders, confirmations, and updates.
  • Improving our website functionality and customer service.
  • complying with legal obligations (e.g., maintaining vehicle service records).

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data. Your personal information is stored on secure Firebase servers (Google Cloud) with encryption standards in place.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us directly.
